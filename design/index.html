Pazator's UI

design language, clean modern black with emotional color accents

Philosophy

Deep blacks and near‑blacks form a neutral, modern canvas. Every section can receive a hit of color that reflects its emotional tone, urgency, calm, creativity, success, or warning.

These accents are never arbitrary: they carry psychological weight and are applied sparingly (borders, badges, glows). The result is an interface that is both minimal and emotionally intuitive.

● urgent ● calm ● creative ● success ● warning
